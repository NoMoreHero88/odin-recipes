World's Best Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for lasagna. It is very good and you should eat it. If it wasn't good, would we call it the world's best lasagna?

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound of lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • a bunch of other stuff that I don't want to type out

Steps

  1. Cooks sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a dutch oven
  2. Boil some water and cook the lasagna noodles in it for 8 to 10 minutes
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  4. Put the meat sauce in the bottom of the dish, put the cheese on top, etc.
  5. Bake in the oven for 25 minutes
