Fresh Salmon Cakes

This is an easy to make salmon cake recipe. Pretty much anyone can make it. Even you.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup minced onion
  • 2 tablespoons of minced celery
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 1/4 pounds of coarsely chopped salmon
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Heat oil over medium heat. Cook and stir onion, red pepper, celery for about 5 minutes.
  2. Stir salmon, onion mixture, mayonnaise, 1/4 cup breadcrumbs in a bowl until well mixed. Cover in bowl and refrigerate for 1-2 hours.
  3. Form salmon mix into 4 1-inch thick patties. Sprinkle remaining bread crumbs on patties.
  4. Cook patties in oil over medium heat until golden and cooked through. About 3-4 minutes per side.
